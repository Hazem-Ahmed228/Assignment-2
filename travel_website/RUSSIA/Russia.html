Russia

Overview

Russia is the largest country in the world, stretching across Europe and Asia. It’s known for its grand history, beautiful architecture, cold winters, and rich culture. The country offers everything from modern cities to snowy mountains and peaceful countryside.

Main Destinations

Here is a list of some cities

Moscow

The capital city and the heart of Russia.

Saint Petersburg

The cultural capital.

Sochi

A resort city on the Black Sea,Beaches in summer and skiing in winter .

Russian Food

  • Borscht
  • Pelmeni
  • Olivier salad

Cultural Tips for Tourists

  • Russians are formal at first, but very friendly once you get to know them.
  • Handshakes are common when greeting.
  • In churches, dress modestly and women often cover their heads.

Best Time to Visit

  1. May–September: Warm weather, perfect for sightseeing.
  2. December–February: Magical snowy landscapes and winter festivals.
