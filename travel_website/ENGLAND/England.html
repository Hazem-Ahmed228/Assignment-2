England

Overview

England is part of the United Kingdom, located in northwestern Europe. It’s famous for its rich history, royal traditions, beautiful countryside, and vibrant cities. Visitors come to see ancient castles, world-class museums, and iconic landmarks.

Main Destinations

Here is a list of some cities

London

The capital and the heart of England. Attractions: Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, Tower Bridge, London Eye, and the British Museum.

Liverpool

Lively city known for music, sports, and nightlife.

Oxford

Historic university towns.

English Food

  • Fish and chips
  • Full English breakfast
  • Sunday roast
  • Afternoon tea

Cultural Tips for Tourists

  • People are polite and often say “please,” “thank you,” and “sorry.”
  • Queuing (standing in line) is very important — never skip!
  • English people love to talk about the weather.
  • Tipping in restaurants is usually 10–15%.

Best Time to Visit

  1. Spring (March–May): Flowers bloom and weather is mild.
  2. Summer (June–August): Warmest months with festivals and long days.
  3. Autumn (September–November): Beautiful fall colors.
  4. Winter (December–February): Cold but great for Christmas lights and cozy pubs.
